Innovation

This project implements a cutting-edge lightweight deep learning segmentation model specifically designed for crop leaf disease segmentation. The system processes images captured from mobile devices, performs real-time segmentation to identify diseased areas, and classifies the specific disease type with high accuracy.

By integrating GPS location data from uploads, the platform creates an interactive disease map that visualizes the geographical distribution of crop diseases, enabling farmers and researchers to track disease spread patterns and take preventive measures effectively.
